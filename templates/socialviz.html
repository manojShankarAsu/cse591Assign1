Patterns Found and Explaination:

  • From this Social Visualization, we can find that , the more no of Questions Read by the user, the lesser no of Questions is asked by the User. More no of Questions read, higher knowledge , so lesser no of Questions asked
  • We can find that , the more no of Page reads(scrolls) by the user, the lesser no of Questions is asked by the User. Higher no of Scrolls, higher knowledge in the topic
  • We can find that , the more no of Questions reads(scrolls) by the user, the higher no of upvotes by the user. Reputation increases as the no of Question read increases
